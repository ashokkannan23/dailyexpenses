About Us

We are dedicated to providing a reliable and user-friendly platform for managing your daily expenses effectively. Our goal is to simplify financial tracking and ensure data security.

Features Our Users Love

~ Manage Your Expenses Easily

Track and manage expenses effortlessly with support for all major Indian currencies and regional denominations.

~ Effortless Daily Spending Recording

Record your daily expenses with our intuitive and user-friendly entry system.

~ Quick Error Correction

Easily correct any mistakes in your expense records using our simple edit feature.

~ Easy Deletion

Delete any unwanted expense records quickly with our easy delete feature.

~ Transaction Insights

View detailed insights on your spending habits, including which transaction methods you use most and total amounts spent.

~ Secure Your Data

Ensure the security of your expense data with our robust login and logout features to prevent unauthorized access.

~ Password Recovery

Effortlessly reset your password with email verification codes if you ever forget it.

~ Continuous Access

Stay logged in after the first login until you manually log out, ensuring convenient and quick access to your expense records.

~ Data Recovery Assurance

Rest assured that your expense data remains safe; even if you delete the app or change/lose your phone, you can recover your data by simply logging in again.

~ Easy App Deletion

At any time, you can delete the app along with all your expense data whenever you choose.
